### Q2: What are the top 3 regions by sales?
**Generated SQL:**
```sql
    SELECT r.name, SUM(s."Line Total") as TotalSales 
    FROM sales_order s 
    JOIN regions r ON s."Delivery Region Index" = r.id 
    GROUP BY r.name 
    ORDER BY TotalSales DESC 
    LIMIT 3;
    ```
**Response:**
The top 3 regions by sales are:
- Lakewood: $6,595,332.60
- Springfield: $6,330,421.30
- Jackson: $4,010,318.50

### Q3: Show me sales for 'South' region.
**Generated SQL:**
```sql
    SELECT SUM(s."Line Total") 
    FROM sales_order s 
    JOIN regions r ON s."Delivery Region Index" = r.id 
    JOIN state_regions sr ON r.state_code = sr."State Code" 
    WHERE sr.Region = 'South';
    ```
**Response:**
The total sales for the 'South' region are $335,135,011.70.
